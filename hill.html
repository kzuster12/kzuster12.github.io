Lithuania (Klaipeda)

In Klaipeda's center can be found lake and in middle there is this hill. It is just a pretty and nice place. Moreover, it is in some way hidden. Person, who do not know where it is or is not enough adventurous will not find it. Yet, I will tell a hint - when you are at the Klaipeda's old town, then from "Vero Cafe" go by bridge and just go straight, at some point there should be a turn to right. The rest depends from how lucky you are to find it!

I wish you a good luck!

Fun fact: Grasshoper is a symbol of taking new adventures. So, go ahead and explore new places and encounter adventures! ^-^
