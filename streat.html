Latvia (Liepaja)

Liepaja's old town is bigger than it might seem. The main places in old town that people are familiar are city's center and city's central park (yet, not even all tourists or people, who are not from Liepaja, know where it is). The best time to visit Liepaja is spring or start of summer, because if people visit city's central park, they at this time will see many trees blossoming, and in central park there are quite a lot of tree, so it is the best time to enjoy nature. Also, not far away from central park is one particular path that leads to "Gulbju dikis" (direct translation- "Swans' pond"). Nevertheless, at this place is a lot of ducks. Moreover, in middle of pond is pergola.

Additionally, Liepaja contains many old streets, where person, who is interested in more exploring old style houses from different centuries then, should just wander in old town's streets.

Fun fact: Grasshoper is a symbol of taking new adventures. So, go ahead and explore new places and encounter adventures! ^-^
