Take an adventure

Lithuania (Klaipeda)

Klaipeda's old town can amaze by its streets. They lead to different sculptures, like big chess. Also, the surrounding, while walking within old town's streets, can impress. Have you ever wished for something from the bottom of your heart? If yes, then in one of these streets can be found mouse (not easily - for myself to find it takes quite while). Yet, this mouse is special. There is legend that person needs to whisper his or hers wish to this mouse's ear, and she will afterwards bring this wish to God.

Go and tell your biggest wish to mouse!

Fun fact: Grasshoper is a symbol of taking new adventures. So, go ahead and explore new places and encounter adventures! ^-^
