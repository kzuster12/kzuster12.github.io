Take an adventure

Latvia (Liepaja)

People, who visit Liepaja, they usually visit sea, which is at the city's center. Yet, if people also would go to the part of Liepaja called "Karosta" (Direct translation- Warport), they could see a sea that is calm and uninfluenced by marketing. This beach allows seeing seashells and stones with different sizes, shapes, colors, and if person is lucky enough he or she might find some amber. Moreover, many locals are used to walk path of pier.

So, next time you visit Liepaja and you want to see uninfluenced sea, you should look for Karosta's sea.

Fun fact: Grasshoper is a symbol of taking new adventures. So, go ahead and explore new places and encounter adventures! ^-^
